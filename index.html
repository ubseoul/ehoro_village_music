The Sushi Formula

4 ingredients per beat, every time

The Beat-Making Workflow

PREPARATION (Monthly - 1-2 hours)
Download 20-30 samples from Splice ($10/month):
  • 5-7 boom bap loops
  • 5-7 Rhodes chord progressions
  • 3-5 Japanese instrument samples
  • 3-5 video game sounds
  • 2-3 ambient textures
Organize into folders: /DRUMS/, /CHORDS/, /MELODIES/, /TEXTURES/, /BASS/
DAY 1: Gather Ingredients (30 min)
  • Pick 1 drum loop (your EAR chooses best)
  • Pick 1 chord progression (Rhodes or piano)
  • Pick 1-2 texture samples
  • Drop all into DAW
DAY 2: Combine & Layer (45 min)
  • Arrange drum loop (intro, verse, chorus)
  • Layer chord progression with drums
  • Program simple sub bass (root notes, 10 min)
  • Add textures (subtle, 10-15% volume)
  • Create dynamics (remove/add for verse/chorus)
DAY 3: Mix & Polish (45 min)
  • Level balance (everything sitting right)
  • EQ (cut mud 200-400Hz, add air 8-12kHz)
  • Compression (glue mix gently)
  • Saturation (warmth, lo-fi feel)
  • Reverb (space, temple vibe)
  • Add vinyl crackle to master (subtle)
  • Export
The Quality Tests: Before finalizing any beat, it must pass:
  • Winter Test: Does it feel cold, contemplative, peaceful? Temple in snow?
  • Sushi Test: Can you identify the 4 ingredients clearly? Nothing cluttered?
  • France Test: Would a woman in Paris at 2am feel understood?
